My Professional Journey

My Journey began when I enrolled in Fanshawe Colleges Interactive Media Design Program September 2019. I am currently on track to graduate the program in 2022. Going forward I am seeking to develop these skills to apply them to front end development. I am also skilled with the adobe suite programs such as Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, Primer Pro, Indesign, and others. Currently I freelance as a Graphic Illustrator.
